What it's scoring for
Seven dimensions, each 1–10: Emotional Specificity, Imagery, Concept & Cohesion, Compression & Subtext, Prosody & Flow, Originality, Memorability. Overall is weighted — Emotional Specificity and Concept count double.

Path to 10/10 — for each dimension, 1–3 surgical notes. Each names the exact line, the mechanical failure at the word level, and a command to fix it. No rewrites — the writing stays yours.

Grader — Llama is fast and free for quick passes. Switch to Claude Sonnet for the deepest, most honest diagnosis on songs you're serious about.

History saves every grade to this browser automatically. Tap any past run to reopen it without regenerating. Delta vs the previous grade shows on each result.
